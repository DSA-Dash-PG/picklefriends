📋 Event Overview
🕒 Start3:00 PM SHARP
🎾 Courts8 courts, 32 players
⏱ Round12 minutes per game
⏸ Break3 min between games
🔄 Rounds8 total
⏰ Duration~2 hours
⏱ Each Round

• Players decide themselves who serves first

• Play 12 minutes regardless of score

• You will receive a warning at minute 11

• When the 12-minute signal sounds, finish the ball in progress

• If tied when horn blows: do NOT count the final point

🪜 After Each Round

⬆️ Winners move up one court (Court 8 stays)

⬇️ Non-winners move down one court (Court 1 stays)

🔀 When partners move, they split and play with new partners

🟢 Coed teams are preferred over the split requirement

⭐ Game 5 exception: Courts are assigned based on cumulative points from Rounds 1–4. Everyone re-seeds!
🥇 How to Win

🥇 1st: Winners of Court 8 in the final round

🥈 2nd: Non-winners of Court 8 in the final round

🥉 3rd: Winners of Court 7 in the final round

📐 Basic Rules

• Receiving team makes the line calls

• May opt for input from opponents or a do-over

🪆 Gnome Facts
🍄 Ball dribbles over the net and hits the base = do-over!
🚻 Bathroom break? Raise your hand — a proxy (with far less talent) will play in your absence. Hurry back… no pressure!
